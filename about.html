About Me

In April 2010 I obtained my PhD at PUC-Rio. My advisor was Edward Hermann Haeusler. My research areas are Description Logics and Proof Theory. Currently, I am a professor at Applied Math School at Getulio Vargas Foundation.

During my PhD I had two outstanding experiences as International fellow at MS Research, between April and July of 2008 and at SRI International, between February and July of 2009.
